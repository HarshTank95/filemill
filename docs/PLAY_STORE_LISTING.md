## Full description (max 4000 chars)

```
FileMill is a complete PDF and file toolkit that runs entirely on your phone.
Unlike other "free" PDF apps, FileMill never uploads your documents — it ships
with no internet permission at all, so your files physically cannot leave your
device. No account, no ads, no watermark, no catch.

Check for yourself: Settings > Apps > FileMill > Permissions. The list is empty.

24 TOOLS, ALL ON-DEVICE

Convert & compare
• PDF to Word — turn a PDF into an editable .docx, structure and tables rebuilt
• Compare PDFs — spot every added, removed or edited word between two versions
• Metadata Cleaner — see the location, device and author hidden in a file, and remove it
• PDF to Images — export pages as PNG or JPG

Create & capture
• Scan to PDF — auto edge-detection, deskew and enhance
• ID Card to PDF — Aadhaar/PAN/licence front & back at true size on one A4
• Images to PDF — turn photos into a clean PDF
• Extract Text (OCR) — pull text out of scans and photos, on-device
• Searchable PDF — add a hidden text layer to scans so they become searchable

Organize
• Merge, Split, Split to Files, Organize pages, Crop

Annotate & sign
• Sign, Add Text (fill forms), Draw, Highlight, Watermark

Secure & optimize
• Protect with a password (AES-256), Redact (truly destroys hidden text),
  Compress to fit size limits, Metadata Cleaner

WHY FILEMILL

• Truly private — no network permission, nothing is ever uploaded
• Truly free — no ads, no watermark, no subscription, no sign-in
• Fast — everything runs locally, even OCR
• Honest — features are built to do what they say, with clear notes on limits

Your documents are yours. FileMill just gives you the tools — and keeps them on
your device, where they belong.
```
